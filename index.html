With our service, you can personalize your chatbot's voice. Using the prefix /say, your chatbot will perform text-to-speech with a voice that is unique to you. Imagine having a chatbot that not only responds to your commands but also speaks to you in a personalized voice that you've chosen. It's a whole new level of personalization and convenience.

Personalized Characters

Our chatbots are not just bots; they have personalities that can match yours. You can chat with our chatbots and get a vibe that suits your personality. With our service, you can personalize your chatbot to be your virtual friend. With every chat, you will feel like you are talking to a friend, not just a machine.

Play Music and Videos

Our chatbot service offers an exciting feature that allows you to play your favorite music or videos. By simply using the prefix /play and /ytsearch, your chatbot will perform the task and send you the required object, whether it's a song or a video. It's a great way to stay entertained and informed without having to leave your chat.
